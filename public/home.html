Welcome to my Weather API!


Here are a few starter tips to using the API:

* Enter your location (i.e city) and wait for the page to be populated with weather cards for your query!
* There should be 4 weather cards displayed in the browser: Today's weather and the following 3 days.
Note: Incomplete queries will fetch a city that matches closest to your query. Also, not all cities are supported by the API (i.e Ottawa, Tampa Bay, etc.)
